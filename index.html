A soft girl with all the grace of a dancer. From her reserved movements and trained posture, her training in presentation is evident. Her cold, reserved nature is befitting of her noble status, yet her lilac eyes possess gentleness and curiosity. A slight shimmer in her pale skin and crowned with long white locks which seem to glow like moonlight, Asteria possesses a strange, ethereal beauty.

On a normal day, Asteria uses delicate shades of rose, lavender, and coral to define her poorly contrasted features. Her hair, always perfectly combed, curls into loose spirals at the ends. Asteria chooses to clothe herself in cute or elegant outfits, often trimmed with lace and paired with fine accessories. No matter the occasion, her outfits always feature a moon motif, symbolic of her and her power.

Personality

Level-headed and reserved, Asteria is observant of situations and often proves herself to be a good listener. Rarely will she speak with strangers, but when she does, she is direct and concise. But while she says little about herself, she’s quite concerned with the details of what goes on around her. Asteria learned from an early age that knowledge is valuable, and one can keep the upper hand on anyone by knowing more about them than they know about you. Nobles often had ulterior motives, a trait which she believes most people to have, which is why she’s incredibly distrustful of others. Asteria fears emotional harm, and the emotional distance she keeps towards strangers lends her a cold look befitting someone of status.

Asteria was taught to value her image above all else since she was young. As a result, she is quite self-conscious and carefully puts on a refined and sophisticated persona, but she is also easily flustered when someone points out a mistake she has made, or discovers that some of her favourite things are cute or a bit childish.

Once her trust is gained, Asteria’s disposition warms. She is playful with her friends and likes to tease. Her perception skills are put to the good cause of learning how to better help her allies in ways other than magic, such as resources they need, an ear to listen, or gentle touch. However, her inquisitive nature can be prying, and her struggle to open up can result in miscommunications on her end.
