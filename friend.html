Relationships

Severo

Asteria initially disliked Severo for his rough behaviour towards her, but after mistakenly swapping journals with him, she was intrigued by what lay beneath the surface. Asteria slowly learned from then on that Severo was capable of caring, even though he struggled adjusting to human habits.

Asteria made an effort to learn more about Severo, and the two have spent a lot of time together. Asteria cares for him and challenges him when he is being stubborn. After all they've been through together, Asteria feels most comfortable by his side.

Roleplayed by Tashea

Roh

Asteria knows well that Roh is a good healer. As one of Severo's friends, Asteria expects a lot from him. While his abilities are impressive, Asteria is concerned that Roh is too soft, and needs to toughen up in order to hold his own. In their exchanges, Asteria has found that she is a bit more compassionate when Roh is around.

Roleplayed by Moonie-Loonie

Sascha

Asteria is unfamiliar with Sascha, though she is impressed with his hunting abilities.

Roleplayed by PinkXombie

Edelweiss

Asteria wasn't too happy to see a light mage as her new bunkmate, and on top of her devastating experiences with roommates before Edelweiss, she never wanted to get attached, either. Asteria unfairly pushes Edelweiss away, and after seeing the rest of her room disappear, Asteria decided to leave before Edelweiss could.

Roleplayed by kumaokaa-san
